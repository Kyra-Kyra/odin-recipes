Bolognese

Description

This is a recipe for bolognese sauce I have come up with after a lot of experimenting. Its deep flavours and thickness make it one of my favorites

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato paste
  • Full red wine
  • Canned tomato cubes (3 cans)
  • Boullion cubes
  • Fresh basil
  • Parmesan
  • Bell peppers
  • Large carrots
  • Celery
  • Cumin
  • Paprika powder
  • Nutmeg
  • Oregano
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Spaghetti noodels

Steps:

  1. Crush the garlic and dice the onions.
  2. Add the garlic and onions to a large stew pan and fry on a low temperature for a few minutes.
  3. Meanwhile cut the bell peppers, carrots and celery into small pieces. Also cut the fresh basil untill its very small.
  4. When the few minutes are over, add the ground beef to the pan and raise the temperature to medium/high.
  5. Add nutmeg, paprika powder, cumin, salt and pepper to taste.
  6. When the ground beef is light brown, add the bell pepper, carrot and celery.
  7. Cook for about 5 more minutes on medium/high.
  8. Add the tomato paste to the pan and cook 2 more minutes.
  9. Add about 200ml of the red wine to the pan and cook for 2 more minutes.
  10. Add the boullion cube and immediately add the canned tomato cubes.
  11. Add the fresh basil and oregano.
  12. Put on low and simmer for 2 to 4 hours,
  13. When ready, cook the spaghetti noodels. Serve the bolognese with the spaghetti noodels and parmesan
