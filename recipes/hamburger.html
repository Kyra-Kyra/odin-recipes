Hamburger

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious homemade hamburger. Pair it with a bun, toppings and sides of your choice! Recipe is for 4 hamburgers.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef 500g
  • Cumin
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Soy sauce
  • Egg
  • Bread crumbs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Onions
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika powder
  • Oregano

Steps:

  1. Slice onions very small.
  2. Throw all of the ingredients into a large bowl.
  3. Mix with your hands until even and firm. Add bread crumbs until you can easily mold it with your hands.
  4. Squash the balls until you get the desired hamburger shape.
  5. Heat butter or oil in a large pan.
  6. Fry the burgers on high heat until both sides are brown.
  7. Lower heat and cook until the burgers are reasonably firm.
  8. Take the burgers out of the pan and wrap them in aluminium foil.
  9. Let the burgers rest for about 5 minutes.
  10. Serve!
