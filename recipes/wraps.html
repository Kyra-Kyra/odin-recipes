Wraps

Description

This is a very simple recipe for ground beef wraps

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef 500g.
  • Package of burrito seasoning.
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 8 large wraps
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Sour cream
  • Lettuce
  • Grated cheese

Steps:

  1. Dice the onions, garlic and bell pepper.
  2. Fry onions and garlic in a pan.
  3. Add ground beef.
  4. Bake untill ground beef is golden brown.
  5. Add bell peppers.
  6. Add seasoning packet.
  7. Follow the instructions on the wraps' packaging to heat the wraps.
  8. Serve with the sour cream, lettuce and grated cheese.
